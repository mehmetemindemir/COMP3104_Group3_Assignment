--------- Importance of learning DevOps in the SDLC ---------
Learning DevOps is important because it bridges the gap between development and operations, enabling faster, more reliable software delivery. It promotes collaboration, automation, and continuous improvement—helping teams build, test, and deploy code more efficiently. By mastering DevOps, you gain skills that boost productivity, reduce errors, improve scalability, and make you highly valuable in today’s tech-driven world.

--- Benefits of Taking this Course ---
- Bridges the Gap Between Development and Operations
- Enables Faster Delivery
- Improves Reliability and Quality
- Increases Scalability and Efficiency
