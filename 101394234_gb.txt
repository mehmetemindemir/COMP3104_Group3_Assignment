--- Introduction ---
I am currently studying at George Brown College, a leading public post-secondary institution situated in the heart of downtown Toronto. 
Being so close to Canada’s financial centre and thousands of industry partners gives me access to a thriving community of students, employees, alumni and donors with deep connections to the city and beyond.
For over 50 years George Brown has helped students like me reach our career and personal goals through innovative programming, applied skills development, and knowledgeable faculty. This environment has been instrumental in shaping my academic and professional growth.

--- Fun Facts ---
As a nationally ranked research college and an accredited member of Polytechnics Canada, George Brown is at the forefront of cutting-edge research and innovation projects. Since 2007, the College has collaborated on 1,998 projects, provided 17,206 student research experiences, and attracted $90 million in research funding — opportunities that students like me can be part of during our studies.
