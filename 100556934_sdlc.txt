--------- Importance of learning DevOps in the SDLC ---------

Regardless of the type of DevOps toolchain an organization uses, 
a DevOps process needs to use the right tools to  address the key phases 
of the DevOps lifecycle:

- Discover
- Plan
- Build
- Test 
- Monitor 
- Operate
- Continuous feedback
